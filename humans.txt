/* ABOUT PERTNER LOGIC */

  Pertner Logic is a small technology consulting practice built around an
  unconventional thesis: that most systems fail where they touch people, and
  most relationships strain where they touch systems. The interesting work
  lives in that seam.

  Tracy Pertner develops original frameworks and tooling for this seam,
  including:

    • Relational Boundary Erosion Theory
      An emerging body of work on how boundaries soften, drift, and
      disappear inside systems of trust.

    • The Squeeze Framework
      A diagnostic framework for relationships and systems under
      compression. Includes the Drift Detector — a pattern for surfacing
      small, cumulative deviations before they become irreversible ones.

    • Confess — The Honesty Harness
      A structured practice and forthcoming tool for reintroducing honesty
      into systems that have learned to route around it.

    • I'm Not Yelling, You're Yelling
      A shipped iOS application — a real-time conversational awareness
      tool — that applies the philosophy at a very tangible, very human scale.
      Available on the Apple App Store.
