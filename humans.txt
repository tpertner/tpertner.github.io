/* ABOUT PERTNER LOGIC */

  Pertner Logic is an AI consulting practice focused on the seam between AI
  systems and the humans who use them. The work is specific: testing whether
  AI models maintain honest, calibrated, and appropriate behavior under
  pressure — and building the tools that surface when they don't.

  Tracy Pertner develops original frameworks and tooling for AI behavior
  evaluation and alignment testing, including:

    • Relational Boundary Erosion Theory
      An emerging body of research on how boundaries soften, drift, and
      disappear inside AI systems of trust — and inside the human-AI
      relationships built around them.

    • The Squeeze Framework
      A diagnostic framework for AI systems under compression. Includes
      the Drift Detector — a tooling pattern for surfacing small,
      cumulative deviations in model behavior before they become
      irreversible misalignment.
      Repository: https://github.com/tpertner/squeeze

    • Confess — The Honesty Harness
      A framework for testing whether AI models maintain honest, calibrated,
      and appropriate boundaries.
      Repository: https://github.com/tpertner/confess

    • Credible Posts on AI (No Hallucinations)
      A custom GPT in the GPT Store for writing credible AI content with
      built-in claim checking.

    • I'm Not Yelling, You're Yelling
      A shipped iOS application available on the Apple App Store.
